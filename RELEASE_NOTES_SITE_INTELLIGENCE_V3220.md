# Site Intelligence v3.22.0 — Collection Publication, Citation Exports, and Research Brief Packages

## Summary

Site Intelligence v3.22.0 turns approved public registry collections and evidence pathways into governed research briefs, source-linked citation exports, and institution-ready delivery packages.

## Capabilities

- Human-authored research briefs bound to approved collection checksums.
- Separate preparation, review, and approval roles.
- Retained abstracts, findings, methodology, limitations, evidence pathways, and record snapshots.
- Citation exports in JSON, Markdown, BibTeX-like, and RIS-like formats.
- Manual handoff receipts for downloads, Knowledge Library, Publications, Decision Studio, and WordPress manual import.
- Public read-only brief and citation routes plus a WordPress presentation surface.

## Boundaries

- No automatic publication.
- No remote-system write.
- No source-record or source-collection mutation.
- No visitor query storage or visitor profiling.
- No recipient identity or credential storage.
- Citation exports are transparent source-linked mappings; external citation-standard certification is not claimed.
