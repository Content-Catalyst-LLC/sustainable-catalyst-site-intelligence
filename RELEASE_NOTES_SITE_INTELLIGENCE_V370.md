# Site Intelligence v3.8.0 — Homepage Intelligence Gateway

## Purpose

Turn Live Intelligence into a calm, traceable homepage entry point for Sustainable Catalyst's connected public-intelligence platform.

## Delivered

- Dedicated public homepage feed with an eight-signal default and twelve-signal maximum.
- Eight governed signal families covering Earth systems, energy and carbon, economics and development, humanitarian conditions, science and environment, institutional indicators, platform operations, and publications and evidence.
- Structured geography for global, regional, country, and local signals.
- Canonical primary and secondary destinations for context, workspaces, evidence, maps, Decision Studio, and source pages.
- WordPress routes for the homepage feed and public gateway policy.
- Homepage auto-render preset limited to eight signals.
- Frontend destination and family metadata for analytics and accessible navigation.

## Boundaries

- Signal families organize navigation; they do not imply causation or accuracy.
- Context and evidence are preferred before external source handoff.
- Backend-relative signal context routes remain behind the WordPress public route.
- The homepage surface does not load the full Site Intelligence application.
- Existing freshness, failure handling, reduced-motion, keyboard, mobile, and source-governance controls remain active.
