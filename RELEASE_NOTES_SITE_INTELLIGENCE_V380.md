# Site Intelligence v3.8.0 — Connected Live Intelligence Surface

This release turns Live Intelligence into one governed signal system that can be reused across the Sustainable Catalyst homepage, channels, publications, Knowledge Library, Advisory, Lab, and approved external embeds.

## Delivered

- Eight canonical surface profiles with bounded defaults.
- Public surface directory, policy, definition, feed, and embed-manifest routes.
- Surface-specific family filters and destination allowlists.
- Centralized gateway, rotation, freshness, source, evidence, and aggregate analytics behavior.
- Seven WordPress preset shortcodes plus the existing general shortcode.
- External embed protections against credentials, administrative routes, private metadata, and arbitrary signal injection.
- Explicit prohibition on advertising, sponsored, and affiliate items inside Live Intelligence.

## Preserved

- Same-query last-known-good recovery and honest freshness states.
- Signal validation, duplicate suppression, and expiry handling.
- Human-governed rotation overrides.
- Reduced-motion, keyboard, mobile, static, and no-JavaScript behavior.
- Source lineage, evidence records, context pages, maps, and deeper platform handoffs.
- Aggregate-only public-value measurement without visitor profiles.

## Boundary

A surface may control presentation, filtering, and approved destinations. It cannot change an observation, claim that delayed data is live, bypass source governance, or publish private operational information.
