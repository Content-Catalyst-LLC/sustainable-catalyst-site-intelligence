# Sustainable Catalyst Site Intelligence v2.8.0

## Alerts, Monitoring, and Live Intelligence Streams

This release adds reconnectable public stream snapshots, browser-local alert rules, stateless matching, source-recency monitoring, deterministic digests, and a WordPress monitoring embed. It requires no paid API, message broker, user database, or hosted subscription system.

### Public route

`/app/?view=alerts`

### WordPress shortcode

`[sc_alerts_monitoring_live_intelligence height="1500"]`

### Safeguards

- no fabricated stream records;
- no server-side user tracking or alert-rule persistence;
- no operational emergency claims;
- no individual risk score, investment signal, legal determination, eligibility decision, or targeting recommendation; and
- source identity, dates, freshness, units, and availability remain visible.
