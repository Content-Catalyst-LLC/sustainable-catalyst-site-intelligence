# Sustainable Catalyst Site Intelligence v2.22.0

## Institutional Workspaces, Collaboration, and Review

Site Intelligence v2.22.0 adds optional shared institutional workspaces while preserving the open, account-free public observatory. It introduces explicit analyst, reviewer, publisher, and administrator roles; assignments; comments and review notes; evidence decisions; shared source collections; activity receipts; institutional branding; preview-first retention; and JSON/ZIP workspace archives.

Public endpoints expose only deliberately published public summaries and approved public source collections. Members, assignments, comments, private evidence, review notes, activity logs, and retention receipts remain private.

The release does not provision accounts, replace an identity provider, publish automatically, approve evidence automatically, perform individual tracking, or claim that export packets were written into another platform.
