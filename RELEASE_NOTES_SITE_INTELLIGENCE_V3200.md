# Site Intelligence v3.22.0

## Public Registry Discovery, Evidence Search, and Institutional Profiles

Site Intelligence v3.22.0 adds a bounded public discovery layer above the approved preservation registry, exchange, attestation, challenge, and appeal records introduced in v3.17.0–v3.22.0.

### Included

- Search across approved public institutions, exchanges, attestations, challenges, and appeals.
- Deterministic relevance, alphabetical, and recent-first sorting.
- Filters for institution type, trust profile, jurisdiction, exchange profile, verification method, and governance outcome.
- Public facet counts for discovery interfaces.
- Evidence-linked institutional profiles combining public policy references, attestations, exchange consensus, and governance history.
- WordPress search and profile surface through `[sc_live_intelligence_registry_discovery]`.
- Public policy, status, facets, search, and institution-profile endpoints.

### Privacy and governance boundaries

- Public search queries are not stored.
- Visitor profiles are not created.
- Public discovery results expose no staff identities or internal review reasons.
- Only approved public-safe records are indexed.
- Source records are not mutated.
- No network verification or remote write is performed.
- Registry discovery is not certification, accreditation, ranking, or endorsement.

Search queries are not stored.
