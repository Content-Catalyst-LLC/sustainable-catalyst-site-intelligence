## Demo sequence

1. Open the Global Observatory and change satellite layers.
2. Compare two Earth-observation dates with the swipe control.
3. Open a country profile and inspect an indicator source.
4. Filter live event records and open a source-linked event detail.
5. Compare Kenya and Ghana while preserving dates and missing values.
6. Generate a source-aware public brief and export its manifest.
7. Open Sources and Methods, then save and share the research path.
