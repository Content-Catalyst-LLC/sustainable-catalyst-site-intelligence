# Site Intelligence v1.14.0

## Geospatial Intelligence, Satellite Imagery, and Live Visualization Layer

Adds an interactive public map, NASA GIBS satellite layers, USGS and NASA EONET event feeds, browser heat maps, date controls, legends, accessible table fallbacks, and WordPress shortcodes.

Public endpoints: `/public/geospatial`, `/layers`, `/events`, `/heatmap`, `/satellite`, `/timeline`, `/accessibility`, and `/diagnostics`.

The interface labels live, cached, and fallback-demonstration states. Satellite imagery is contextual and may be delayed, composited, cloud-obscured, or unavailable for a selected date.
