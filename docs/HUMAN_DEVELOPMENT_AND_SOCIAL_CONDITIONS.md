# Human Development and Social Conditions — v1.10.0

This release adds a source-aware public observatory for poverty, inequality, health, education, decent work, food security, and water and sanitation.

## Sources

UN SDG Global Database, World Bank Indicators and PIP, WHO Global Health Observatory and inequality resources, UNESCO UIS, ILOSTAT, FAOSTAT, and UN-Water SDG 6.

## Governance

- Preserve indicator definitions, units, reference periods, revisions, and estimation status.
- Keep disaggregation dimensions when available.
- Do not flatten unlike indicators into a proprietary composite score by default.
- Treat missing data and methodological breaks as visible information, not zero values.
- Do not infer individual eligibility, diagnosis, legal status, or entitlement from aggregate statistics.
