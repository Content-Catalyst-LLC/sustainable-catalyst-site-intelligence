# Site Intelligence v2.7.0 — Unified Country and Regional Intelligence Dossiers

This release adds a public cross-domain dossier workspace that composes existing Site Intelligence evidence without flattening it into one score.

## Public route

`/app/?view=dossiers`

## WordPress shortcode

`[sc_country_regional_intelligence_dossiers height="1500"]`

## Included evidence domains

- Country indicators and trends
- Global conditions and live observations
- Economics, markets, and sustainability
- International law and governance
- Scientific and Earth systems
- Humanitarian, conflict, and displacement
- Trade, energy, and resources

## Methodological boundaries

Country and regional dossiers describe available public evidence. They do not establish performance, completeness, risk, responsibility, legal effect, resilience, vulnerability, humanitarian priority, or national-security status. Statistics, forecasts, legal records, humanitarian reports, scientific observations, and derived analysis remain distinct evidence classes.
