# Sustainable Catalyst Site Intelligence v2.10.0

## Research Paths, Saved Investigations, and Briefing Workflows

Adds a browser-local research workspace for organizing public Site Intelligence evidence into reviewable investigations. Users can maintain research questions, scope, notes, evidence records, checkpoints, and saved public views; validate and export investigations; generate deterministic briefing packets; and create structured handoffs for Knowledge Library, Research Librarian, Workbench, and Decision Studio.

### Privacy and integrity

- No account or hosted user profile is required.
- Investigation history remains in browser storage unless explicitly exported.
- The backend validates and packages records statelessly.
- Evidence classes, units, dates, source links, and authority context are preserved.
- No automatic factual claims, legal conclusions, forecasts, rankings, or recommendations are produced.
- No paid API or persistent queue is required.
