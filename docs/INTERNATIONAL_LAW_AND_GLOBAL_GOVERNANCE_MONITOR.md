# International Law and Global Governance Monitor

Site Intelligence v1.10.0 adds a source-aware public layer for sanctions, treaties, United Nations decisions, international courts, human-rights mechanisms, European Union law, and trade governance.

## Public boundaries

The monitor presents official records and metadata. It does not provide legal advice, sanctions screening, compliance certification, eligibility decisions, or automated judgments about guilt or liability.

## Source and status rules

- Preserve authoritative identifiers and source links.
- Separate publication, adoption, entry into force, amendment, repeal, withdrawal, and finality.
- Distinguish allegations, recommendations, interim measures, orders, judgments, and final decisions.
- Label machine-extracted and derived relationships.
- Require human review for summaries of legal effect or compliance.
