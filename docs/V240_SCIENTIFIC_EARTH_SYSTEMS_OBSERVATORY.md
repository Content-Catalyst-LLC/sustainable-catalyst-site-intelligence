## Interpretation boundaries

Scientific metadata discovery is not peer review, validation, diagnosis, or an automated conclusion. Forecasts are not observations. Remote-sensing products require domain interpretation and, where appropriate, ground validation. Computed material records are not automatically experimental findings.
