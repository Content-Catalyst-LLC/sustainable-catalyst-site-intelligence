# Site Intelligence v3.21.0

## Registry Governance, Challenges, Revocation, and Appeals

Site Intelligence v3.21.0 adds append-only evidence-based governance and appeals for the federated preservation registry.

### Included

- Append-only institution challenges with public-safe evidence references.
- Independent review and separate final approval.
- No-action, trust-profile update, suspension, and revocation decisions.
- Appeals that may uphold, reinstate, or modify a trust profile.
- Public challenge and appeal histories for previously public institutions.
- Current consensus automatically excludes suspended or revoked institutions while preserving their prior attestations.
- JSON and Markdown governance packages.
- Read-only WordPress governance surface.

### Boundaries

- No record deletion or history rewrite.
- No automatic suspension, revocation, or reinstatement.
- No external network verification or remote-system write.
- No credential, personal contact, subscriber, or recipient storage.
- No certification, accreditation, ranking, or endorsement claim.
