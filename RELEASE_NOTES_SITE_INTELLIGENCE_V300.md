# Site Intelligence v3.0.0

## Connected Public Intelligence and Evidence Platform

Site Intelligence v3.0.0 unifies the mature v2.x observatories and governance layers behind one public-safe discovery, context, provenance, and lifecycle interface.

### Added

- Unified public search across countries, regions, events, indicators, datasets, sources, claims, models, investigations, publications, institutional workspaces, and workflow routes.
- Shared context bundles with related public records and recommended product routes.
- Digest-linked provenance chains that preserve source, catalog, and discovery receipts.
- Source-to-publication lifecycle visibility covering ingest, archive, harmonization, analysis, review, publishing, monitoring, and exchange.
- JSON and CSV connected-intelligence exports.
- Dedicated Connected Platform public application workspace.
- Public WordPress shortcode and administrator-only control center.
- Release policy declaring account-free public access, private-record exclusion, human review, conflict preservation, and no hidden ranking or causal inference.

### Boundaries

- Search relevance is lexical discovery, not truth, authority, importance, causation, endorsement, or risk.
- Private records, credentials, and administrative history are excluded.
- Graph proximity, time order, or shared terminology do not establish causation.
- Packet creation does not prove remote delivery.
- The default zero-cost index is generated on request and does not claim a persistent search cluster.
