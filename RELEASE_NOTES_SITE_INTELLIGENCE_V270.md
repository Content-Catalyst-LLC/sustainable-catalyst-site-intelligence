# Sustainable Catalyst Site Intelligence v2.7.0

## Unified Country and Regional Intelligence Dossiers

v2.7.0 introduces a cross-domain public dossier workspace for countries and catalog regions. It composes existing Site Intelligence and Platform Core evidence while preserving source identity, delivery state, reporting date, unit, authority category, quality context, and missing-data boundaries.

### New workspace

- `/app/?view=dossiers`
- Country dossiers
- Regional dossiers based on the normalized country catalog
- Two-country coverage alignment
- Domain coverage matrix
- Source-aware evidence samples
- CSV export and shareable views
- Briefing workflow handoff

### Public endpoints

- `/public/intelligence-dossiers`
- `/public/intelligence-dossiers/facets`
- `/public/intelligence-dossiers/country`
- `/public/intelligence-dossiers/region`
- `/public/intelligence-dossiers/compare`
- `/public/intelligence-dossiers/brief`
- `/public/intelligence-dossiers/diagnostics`

### Safeguards

The release does not create a composite score, country ranking, causal conclusion, legal determination, humanitarian priority, sanctions result, or national-security rating. No paid provider is required and no fallback evidence is fabricated.
