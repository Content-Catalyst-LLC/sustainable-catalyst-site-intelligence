# Site Intelligence v3.15.0

## Public Record Archive, Provenance Ledger, and Long-Term Preservation

This release preserves approved public briefings, publication releases, and public change notices as separately reviewed, append-only archive records.

### Added

- Canonical public-safe source snapshots.
- Source, record, manifest, package, event, and handoff SHA-256 checksums.
- Append-only archive-chain links.
- Permanent, ten-year, and seven-year retention classes.
- Verification that blocks archival approval after source drift.
- Separate preparation, verification, and approval roles.
- JSON and Markdown preservation packages.
- Manual Knowledge Library, Publications, institutional-archive, and download custody handoffs.
- Public policy, status, archive-record, verification, and source-lineage endpoints.
- Read-only WordPress public archive shortcode.

### Boundaries

- No source mutation.
- No archive deletion.
- No destination write.
- No remote institutional deposit.
- No credentials or recipient identities stored.
- No advertising or affiliate content.

The preservation ledger is append-only and every approved record remains linked to its original public source.
