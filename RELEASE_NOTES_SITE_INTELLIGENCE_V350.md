# Site Intelligence v3.5.0 — Topic and Regional Channels

Site Intelligence v3.5.0 adds reusable Live Intelligence channels without creating a second feed system.

## Included

- Topic channels for Earth systems, weather and climate, humanitarian conditions, economy/energy/resources, science and research, and infrastructure/resilience.
- Regional channels for Africa, the Americas, Asia-Pacific, Europe, and the Middle East and North Africa.
- Explicit country and region filters using source-supplied metadata.
- Public channel directory, channel detail, channel policy, and channel feed routes.
- WordPress default-channel controls and shortcode `channel`, `region`, and `country` attributes.
- Channel match reasons attached to selected signals.
- Honest empty results with no silent global fallback.

## Preserved

Feed controls, source operations, event clustering, transparent ranking, signal context, evidence downloads, mobile rotator behavior, placement reliability, caching, and Astra/theme navigation and breadcrumb styling remain intact.

## Boundaries

Channels are routing and filtering tools. They do not certify truth, infer missing geography from coordinates, make geopolitical judgments, or claim exhaustive coverage.
